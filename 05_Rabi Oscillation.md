Simple simulation demonstrating Rabi oscillations and the impact of decoherence on qubit dynamics.

Overview:

This project visualizes:

1. Ideal Rabi oscillations with no decoherence
2. Realistic Rabi oscillations affected by T2 dephasing
3. Exponential decay of oscillation amplitude over time
4. The coherence envelope that limits qubit control fidelity
   
Output:



The program:

1. Plots ideal and decoherence-limited Rabi oscillations
2. Displays the T2 decay envelope
3. Prints key simulation parameters and physics insights

   
Key Concepts:

1. Rabi Frequency (Ω): Determines how quickly a qubit oscillates between |0⟩ and |1⟩ states
2. T2 Dephasing Time: Determines how long quantum coherence is preserved
3. Longer T2 values allow more coherent qubit operations and higher gate fidelity

Learning Objective:

Understand how qubits are controlled through Rabi oscillations and how decoherence limits practical quantum computing performance.
